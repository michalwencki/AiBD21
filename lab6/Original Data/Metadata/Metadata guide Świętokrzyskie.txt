Material first obtained on 05.11.2021.
Material obtained by downloading a repository of Analiza i Bazy Danych exercise.
The data describes sales of RTV and AGD product in the Świętokrzyskie region in Poland,
every line in the data represents an item sold, additionally described by:
- number of days since purchase
- the name of the company which created the product
- the age of the buyer
- the sex of the buyer
- rating of the product
